About

At the intersection of entrepreneurship, technology & investment.

I'm a venture architect and capital advisor focused on building businesses that create measurable economic impact. As Co-founder of OutsourceToBD.com, I'm on a mission to multiply Bangladesh's software and technology exports by connecting global buyers with trusted Bangladeshi technology companies — building the country's trusted gateway for software outsourcing and positioning Bangladesh as a leading global technology destination.

Alongside this, I serve as Entrepreneur in Residence (EIR) at Brain Station 23, working on venture creation, strategic partnerships, innovation initiatives, and ecosystem development with one of Bangladesh's leading software companies.

Over the past decade, I've worked with startups, SMEs, multinationals, and investors across business strategy, venture building, go-to-market execution, capital advisory, product strategy, and international market expansion — helping founders build scalable companies and creating bridges between Bangladesh and global innovation ecosystems.
